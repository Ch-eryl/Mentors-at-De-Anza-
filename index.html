About Us

Mentors@De Anza is a student-run peer mentorship program under the Office of College Life at De Anza College. We aim to connect any current student who seeks help with experienced students who will share resources for navigating De Anza. This includes academic, extracurricular, and transfer mentorship.

Your mentor can point you to programs that can enhance your De Anza experience, and cheer you on as you find your place at De Anza. Our ultimate goal is to foster a supportive community by providing mentorship opportunities and other resources to students. In this long academic journey, you are not alone and we can help you!

Common Questions

Who can join the Mentors Program?

Any current De Anza student interested in connecting, learning, and helping peers. Both mentors and mentees must be enrolled in at least 6 units at De Anza College during the current quarter.

How are mentors and mentees paired?

We match based on major, interests, and goals through our online application. Our matching algorithm considers academic pathways, career interests, and personal preferences to create the most beneficial mentor-mentee relationships.

Do I need prior experience to be a mentor?

No! We provide comprehensive training and ongoing support to help you become an amazing mentor. The most important qualities are a willingness to help others and having completed at least two quarters at De Anza.

How much time commitment is required?

Mentors typically spend 2-3 hours per week, including a 1-hour meeting with their mentee and program activities. Mentees commit to attending scheduled meetings and program events. The program runs for the entire quarter.

What benefits do mentors receive?

Mentors develop leadership skills, build their resume, receive priority registration for certain events, earn volunteer hours, and get letters of recommendation. Plus, the satisfaction of making a positive impact on fellow students!

Can I request a specific mentor or mentee?

Yes! On the application form, you can indicate if you have a specific person in mind. If both parties agree, we'll pair you together while still providing all the program benefits and support.

Still have questions? Contact us at mentors@deanza.edu
